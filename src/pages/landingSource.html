The Benefits of SingleCare

Works with insurance – It’s common for health insurance to limit some services. In these cases, use SingleCare for great cash rates on healthcare services.

Great dental discounts – No dental insurance? No problem. Use SingleCare to find a local dentist at the right price.

No membership fees – Unlike insurance there’s no monthly fee. You’ll ONLY pay for the care you receive.

Discounted rates – When you visit a SingleCare doctor, you can be sure you’re getting a great price.

Great Rx prices – SingleCare includes savings on all prescription medications at a pharmacy near you!

Broad network – Enjoy discounts on nearly all out-patient medical specialties.
